Recovery Strategy — Honest Assessment
📊 Your Situation
Add your holdings to see a personalized assessment.
Option 1 — Hold and Wait
✅
Works if: You hold fundamentally strong projects that will recover. No stress, no action needed. Just patience.
⚠️
Risk: Some projects never recover. If you are holding weak projects, holding is just a slow loss. The market recovering does not mean every coin recovers.
📏
Rule: Only hold projects where you understand the fundamentals and believe in the team and use case. If you cannot explain why you own it — consider exiting.
Option 2 — Active Rotation
✅
Works if: The market is recovering. You rotate into strength and back into your core holding. You accumulate more coins with the same capital.
⚠️
Risk: Rotation done emotionally or randomly makes things worse. You sell your best coin right before it moves. You buy the wrong one at the top.
📏
Rules for safe rotation: Never sell more than 30% of any position. Only rotate into coins that are showing volume and momentum on the scanner. Always have a buy-back plan before you sell.
✅ The Rotation Rules
1️⃣
Scanner first — only rotate into a coin that is showing volume signal on your scanner. Not hope. Not feeling. Volume.
2️⃣
Never sell your full position — maximum 30% of any holding. You keep 70% of your base. If the rotation goes wrong you still own most of your coins.
3️⃣
Know your re-entry before you sell — decide the exact price you will buy back before you click sell. Write it down. Stick to it.
4️⃣
Goal is more coins, not more dollars — you are not trying to get rich in one trade. You are trying to own more coins so when the recovery comes, you recover more.
5️⃣
BTC and market direction first — if BTC is falling, do not rotate into anything. Wait. Rotating into a falling market accelerates your losses.
⚠️ The Honest Truth
💭
Most people in this situation make it worse — not better. They trade emotionally trying to recover losses. They take bigger risks. They end up with $500 instead of $2,000.
🧘
The $2,000 you have is real capital — protect it first. Growing it from $2,000 back to $10,000 requires 5x. That is achievable with patience and the right strategy. Losing the $2,000 means starting over.
📈
The market has already done most of the damage — markets that fall 75% historically recover. The question is not if but when and which coins survive. Focus on quality holdings.
